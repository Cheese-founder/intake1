Rick Astley fanpage.

This is a fanpage for Rick astley fans. Richard Paul Astley is an English singer-songwriter, musician and radio host. He is best known for his debut single Never Gonna Give You Up from 1987. Which became a number 1 hit in 25 countries Rick Astley is the only male solo artist whose first 8 singles all made it into the Top 10 of the UK Singles Chart. And by 1993 he had sold approximately 40 million records worldwide.
